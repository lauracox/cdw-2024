The Charlottesville Design Week is completely volunteer-led and has two simple goals:

  1. Develop a strong design community in central Virginia
  2. Convince the general public that good design is worth a damn

Whether you push pixels, press ink, set type, build environments, or do anything else design-related, you will feel right at home during our growing festival.

Schedule

Tuesday 9

  • Opening Meetup

    Kick off the festivities with Tuesday Design Society’s weekly meetup. Introduce yourself, bring a design to show off, and get to know other local designers and creatives.

    Time........6:00–7:30pm

    Location....Vault Virginia

    Cost........Free!

  • Film Screening and director Q&A: Linotype: The Film

    Linotype: The Film is a feature-length documentary centered around the Linotype type casting machine. Called the “Eighth Wonder of the World” by Thomas Edison, it revolutionized printing and society. The film tells the charming and emotional story of the people connected to the Linotype and how it impacted the world.

    Learn More

    Time........7:30–9:00pm

    Location....Vault Virginia

    Cost........Free!

Wednesday 10

  • Design Marathon

    Donate a day of your creative juices to a local non-profit. Participants form several “agencies-for-a-day,” working with a variety of designers, copywriters, and developers to satisfy the beneficiary's brief.

    Learn More

    Time........8:30am–4:30pm

    Location....Vault Virginia

    Cost........Free!

  • Design Marathon Showcase

    See the Design Marathon agencies present their work to the non-profit. It’s like the best scenes from Mad Men, but live and in person. Not to mention, the selected design will be put to use by the non-profit for years to come.

    Time........6:00–7:00pm

    Location....Vault Virginia

    Cost........Free!

  • Film Screening: Squaring the Circle

    Celebrated filmmaker Anton Corbijn tells the story of Hipgnosis, the iconic album art design studio. Featuring brand-new interviews with Paul McCartney, Roger Waters, Robert Plant, Noel Gallagher, and more.

    Learn More

    Time........7:15–9:00pm

    Location....Vault Virginia

    Cost........Free!

Thursday 11

  • Workshops

    Learn a thing or two! We have workshops covering light photography, design thinking practices, letterpress printing, and more!

    Learn More

    Time........9:30am–5:00pm

    Location....Various

    Cost........$30

  • Open Houses

    A showcase of multiple local design firms opening their doors for attendees to experience their work environments, learn about their projects, and network with fellow design professionals.

    Learn More

    Time........9:30am–5:00pm

    Location....Various

    Cost........Free!

  • Portfolio Review

    Present your portfolio to a panel of expert judges... in front of a crowd of peers. Don’t worry, though, it’s more Great British Baking Show than American Idol.

    Time........5:30–7:30pm

    Location....Vault Virginia

    Cost........Free!

  • Film Screening: Urbanized

    Urbanized is a feature-length documentary about the design of cities, which looks at the issues and strategies behind urban design and features some of the world’s foremost architects, planners, policymakers, builders, and thinkers.

    Learn More

    Time........7:30–9:00pm

    Location....Vault Virginia

    Cost........Free!
