Tour local design firms and workspaces.

Take a self-guided tour through Charlottesville’s downtown design firms. Visit the agencies’ offices, hear about their work, and see the available creative opportunities.

Schedule

9:30 – 11:30AM

  • 618 Forest St, Ste A

    Watermark

    Watermark Design is an award-winning branding and package design studio delivering exceptional artistry.

    See their work

  • 300 E Main St

    Vault Virginia

    Vault Virginia is a membership-based workspace and social club for creators, innovators and thought-leaders. Their carefully designed office, coworking, gallery, and event spaces inspire creativity, cultivate curiosity, and promote connection.

    Learn more

  • 418 4th St NE

    Journey Group

    Journey Group is an independent design company whose studios design solutions that advance the missions of their partners.

    See their work

  • 233 4th St NW

    Virginia Center for the Book

    The Virginia Center for the Book works across the Commonwealth to unite communities of readers, writers, artists, and book lovers. The main office, near Downtown, is also home to the largest publicly-available collection of movable type in the state.

    Learn more

12:30 – 2:30PM

  • 946 Grady Ave Ste 100

    MOI

    Established in 1983, MOI is a commercial furniture, architectural walls, and interior solutions provider that furnishes any space from floor to ceiling. See their work at the Virginia Humanities office, in Diary Central.

    See their work

  • 333 2nd St SE

    10 Flavors Studios

    10 Flavors Studios is a creative cooperative near the Downtown Mall. It is home to designers, coders, and makers of all kinds. The entrance door is directly to the left of the Sultan Kebab door. Climb the stairs up to the second floor. There is a "Ten Flavors" sign on the door.

    Learn more

3:00 – 5:00PM

  • 1835 Broadway St

    WillowTree

    WillowTree is one of the best web and mobile app development companies in the world. Founded in Charlottesville in 2008, they are a leading employer of designers in the area and a global thought leader in the space.

    See their work

  • 802 East Jefferson St

    Suite B3

    Design Develop

    Design Develop is a collaborative firm that combines construction and design expertise to solve complex challenges in a service-oriented manner with open communication.

    See their work

  • 110 5th St SE

    Iconograph

    Iconograph is a creative studio designing meaningful branded environments that navigate, educate, and inspire users.

    See their work

  • 200 East Market St

    VMDO

    Headquartered in Charlottesville, VMDO is the largest architecture firm in central Virginia with internationally-recognized designs of learning environments and high performance buildings across the U.S.

    See their work
